Using the BlogBridge Service

BlogBridge, the application you are running, uses a Web based, free, service, to Provide many of its coolest features. As a user, most of this is behind the scenes and invisible and behind to you. Here are the things you need to know:

  1. The Tools/BlogBridge Service... dialog box is your control center for the BlogBridge Service. This is where you can set and change your password, as well as control synchronization.
  2. The BlogBridge service is free. However you do need to sign up and activate it. When you sign up (and provide an email and a password) you will receive a confirmation email in your inbox. Once you click on the link, you are registed. Note: If you don't click on this link, the service is not activated, and BlogBridge runs in a stand-alone mode with slightly less functionality.
  3. One of the handy things the service does for you is synchronization, which makes it easy to use BlogBridge from different computers, by storing your personal setup, Guides etc on the service. On the Synchronization tab of the Tools/BlogBridge Service... dialog you will see a Synchronize button. Click this button for a two-way synch, or the little arrow button next to it for a one way synch.
