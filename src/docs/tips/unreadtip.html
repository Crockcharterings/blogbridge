How does Unread work?

BlogBridge keeps track of which articles you have read already. Articles which are new (unread) are displayed with a bold title. Feeds containing unread articles also are displayed with bold titles. This makes it easy to quickly locate information you might want to look at.

There are several additional elements to the Unread feature in BlogBridge.

  • In Preferences, you can indicate the number of seconds you should linger on an article to consider it to be read
  • You can also ask that all articles in a Channel automatically be marked read as soon as you move on to another Channel
  • You can even ask that all Channels and Articles in a Channel Guide be marked read as soon as you move on to another Guide.
  • There are commands to navigate directly to the next unread article, or just the next unread in the current Channel Guide, or the next unread Article in the current Channel.

Also remember that when you Synch with the BlogBridge Service, unread state is also synchronized!
